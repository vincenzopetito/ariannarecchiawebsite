About me

Hi there!
My name is Arianna

I'm an Italian born, Digital Product Designer based in London, UK. I'm passionate about everything design and learning new techniques on 3D typography and prototyping.

At Bitstocks I spend most of my time in strategy sessions, product reviews, product updates, retrospectives, usability testing sessions, QA-ing, prototyping, interviewing users and much more...

In my spare time you can find me planning my next trip, binging Netflix, obsessing over crime podcasts, attempting to learn to play piano or lost in my thoughts while listening to music.

Two of my favourite activities at work involve creating smooth animations and sticking post-its on white boards.
