About me

I’m Arianna, a Digital Designer based in London.

I collaborate with inspiring brands, businesses & start-ups to shape their digital projects. My aim is to craft purposeful brands with a holistic approach so that you look and perform your best. I believe that we should design our lives for good: socially, environmentally, and with accessibility in mind. I seek to work with like-minded people who are endlessly passionate about what they do.

As for a few things that I like more than the things mentioned above, Sci-fi and fantasy films and anything Harry Potter related are some. I must admit that I would drop my career in an instant if Hogwarts would send me a letter. Still nothing in the post.
