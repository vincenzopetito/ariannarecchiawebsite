I am here to help
  1. 1

    How did I get involved in digital design?

    At the age of 6, when I was first introduced to the digital tools Macromedia Freehand and Corel Draw. After that magical experience, I was constantly playing with them on my dad’s Win 95 computer. In 2008, I studied graphic design and art direction at NABA in Milan, graduated in 2013. In the same year, with much-improved skills, I got my first job as a designer!
  2. 2

    What drives me?

    My forte lies in combining user, business, and development goals to develop relevant user-centered ideas and prototypes.  My job as a designer is more than just constructing eye-catching, easy-to-use interfaces. It is more about closing the gap between people and their goals with inclusive and empathetic creative solutions.
  3. 3

    What am I good at?

    Minimalist principles are the basis to my work. I have a passion for balance and harmony. I am obsessed with typography and combining it with the latest trends (currently gradients with noise!). I am inherently talented at spotting misaligned elements and creating pixel-perfect design solutions.
